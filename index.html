My Story

I'm Joseph, the runt of my litter, born in a small, peaceful town. I was small but had style – I wore cool sunglasses on my fluffy white face.

People loved watching me strut down the cobblestone streets with my shades on. I explored the town, made friends, and taught everyone that size doesn't matter; it's your spirit that counts.

One summer, I entered a talent competition at the town fair. In the spotlight, I danced gracefully, my sunglasses never slipping. The crowd cheered, and I became a local legend, proving that being small and unique can be a powerful thing.
